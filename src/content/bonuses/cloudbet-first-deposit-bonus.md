CloudBet matches your first deposit 100%, up to 5 BTC. That cap is one of the highest you will see in crypto gambling.

The catch is how the bonus releases. It does not land in your balance all at once. You earn loyalty points through play, and the bonus unlocks in pieces as you accumulate points.

## How the Bonus Release Works

For every bet you place, CloudBet awards loyalty points. Once you hit enough points, a portion of your bonus converts to real funds. Those funds carry zero wagering. You withdraw them right away.

This is different from a standard rollover model. You are not grinding through 40x wagering on the full bonus. Instead, you chip away at it over time.

## The Math

The release rate depends on the game. Slots earn points fastest. Sports betting and table games earn at a slower rate.

Roughly, for every 800 loyalty points earned, 0.001 BTC of your bonus unlocks. That scales linearly.

| Deposit | Bonus (100%) | Points Needed (total) |
| ------- | ------------ | --------------------- |
| 0.1 BTC | 0.1 BTC      | ~80,000               |
| 1 BTC   | 1 BTC        | ~800,000              |
| 5 BTC   | 5 BTC        | ~4,000,000            |

Larger deposits mean more grinding. Plan accordingly.

## Is This Good?

If you are a high-volume player, this is one of the strongest first-deposit bonuses available. The 5 BTC cap is generous. The loyalty-release model means no panic to clear wagering before an expiry date.

The main downside is speed. It takes a lot of action to fully unlock the bonus. Smaller depositors will release a fraction of it before losing interest.

For a 0.1 BTC deposit, the bonus works well. For 5 BTC, you need to be playing daily for months.
