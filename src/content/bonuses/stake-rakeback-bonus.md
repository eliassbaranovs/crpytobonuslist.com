Stake does not do a traditional welcome bonus. Instead, you get rakeback from day one. Every bet you place returns a small percentage straight to your balance.

The rakeback percentage depends on your VIP tier. New accounts start at the base level. The more you play, the higher your return rate.

## How Rakeback Works at Stake

Every wager you make across casino and sports gets tracked. The house edge portion of each bet triggers a rakeback credit. You do not need to lose for it to apply. Winning bets count too.

Rakeback accrues in real time. You claim it manually from your account page whenever you want. No minimum threshold.

## VIP Tiers and Rates

Stake runs a multi-tier VIP system. Progression is based on total wagered amount. Here is a rough breakdown of what each level returns:

| VIP Level | Approx. Rakeback | Extras             |
| --------- | ---------------- | ------------------ |
| Bronze    | ~5%              | Rakeback only      |
| Silver    | ~7%              | Weekly bonus       |
| Gold      | ~9%              | Weekly + monthly   |
| Platinum  | ~11%             | All bonuses + host |
| Diamond   | ~15%+            | Custom deals       |

Exact percentages are not published officially. These are community-reported numbers. Your mileage will vary depending on what games you play.

## What This Means in Practice

If you wager €10,000 at Bronze level, you get roughly €500 back regardless of whether you end up or down. That adds up over weeks and months.

Compare that to a 100% match bonus with 40x wagering. The match looks bigger upfront, but a consistent 5-15% return on everything you bet is solid long-term math.

## Claiming

Go to your Stake dashboard. Click the VIP section. You will see your accrued rakeback. Hit claim and it goes to your main balance. No wagering on rakeback funds.

That last part is the key advantage. Rakeback winnings are withdrawable immediately.

## Who This Works For

Rakeback is the better deal if you play regularly. One-time depositors will not see the value because the returns are small per bet.

If you put through €50,000+ per month, the rakeback at Platinum or Diamond level outperforms any standard welcome bonus on the market.

Casual players are better off taking a match bonus elsewhere and coming back to Stake when their volume picks up.
