# Daniela Voss

**iGaming Bonus Analyst**

Daniela Voss is an iGaming compliance specialist turned bonus reviewer. After six years working on the operator side — first at a Malta-licensed casino group, then at a payment processor — she switched to player advocacy. She reverse-engineers bonus structures, stress-tests wagering scenarios, and flags predatory terms before readers commit their deposits.

## Credentials

- M.Sc. Financial Mathematics, TU Munich
- Former Compliance Officer at LeoVegas Group
- MGA Personal Management Licence (inactive)

With over 6 years of industry experience.

## Review Methodology

Every bonus is evaluated through a five-point framework: (1) effective wagering cost per euro of bonus, (2) game contribution fairness, (3) cashout cap vs. expected value, (4) time pressure and expiry traps, (5) transparency of T&Cs. I simulate 1,000 wagering sessions mathematically to estimate the real expected return before rating any offer.
