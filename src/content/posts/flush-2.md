### Licensing and Security

Flush Casino operates under a complex licensing structure that warrants careful consideration. The casino is owned by King of Clubz B.V., a company registered in Curacao and operating under a Curacao e-gaming license. This is a very common license in the crypto casino industry, known for its accessibility to operators but offering limited player protection and dispute resolution compared to top-tier regulators like the Malta Gaming Authority (MGA) or the UK Gambling Commission (UKGC).

Similar to the Kahnawake Gaming Commission, it is a sovereign regulator that operates independently of Canadian federal law. While legitimate, its standards for player protection, responsible gambling, and operator oversight are not as stringent or well-established as those of major European regulators.
