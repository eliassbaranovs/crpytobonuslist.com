## Wagering Requirements and Conditions

The wagering requirements at Crypto-Games.io are structured in a tiered system that decreases with each subsequent deposit. The first deposit bonus is subject to a 40x playthrough requirement, the second requires 35x, and the third drops to 25x. The fine print reveals a crucial detail: this multiplier is applied to your deposit amount, not the bonus funds. This is an uncommon structure that changes the calculation significantly.

Let us do the math. For the first deposit, if you deposit $100, you receive a $100 bonus. The wagering requirement is calculated on your deposit: $100 x 40 = $4,000. This is equivalent to a 40x bonus wagering requirement for a 100% match, which is on the higher side of the industry average of around 35x. For the second 50% match bonus, a $100 deposit gets you a $50 bonus, but the wagering is still based on the deposit: $100 x 35 = $3,500. This makes the effective wagering on the bonus funds a steep 70x ($3,500 / $50), which is very difficult to clear.

Several other conditions complicate matters. The most significant is the maximum cashout rule, which caps your total withdrawable balance at the sum of your deposit and the bonus received. If you deposit $100, get a $100 bonus, and win $1,000 after completing wagering, you can only withdraw $200. This severely limits the upside potential of any large win. Furthermore, play on Live Casino and Mini Games is prohibited while a bonus is active, and a specific list of slot games is also excluded. The casino does offer a generous max bet limit of $15, which is higher than the typical $5 cap at many other sites.
