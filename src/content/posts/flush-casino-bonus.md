## Wagering Requirements and Conditions

The wagering requirements at Flush Casino are the most important detail to understand. Here is what the T&Cs actually say: the playthrough applies to the sum of your deposit and the bonus you receive. This significantly increases the total amount you need to bet compared to bonuses that only require wagering on the bonus funds.

For Tier 1, the requirement is 30x on deposit plus bonus. Let's run a quick example. If you deposit $100, you get a $100 bonus. Your total starting balance is $200. The total wagering you must complete is ($100 Deposit + $100 Bonus) x 30, which equals $6,000. This is effectively a 60x wagering requirement on the bonus amount alone, which is on the high side of the industry average.

For Tier 2, the requirement is 35x on deposit plus bonus. If you deposit $500, you get a $750 bonus (150% match), giving you a total of $1,250. The real cost of this bonus is calculated as ($500 Deposit + $750 Bonus) x 35, which comes to a massive $43,750 in total wagers. While the 150% match is excellent, the volume of play required is substantial. This is a common structure for high-percentage bonuses, but you need to be aware of the commitment.

During the wagering period, your maximum bet is capped at $5 per round for both tiers. Be careful not to exceed this, as doing so can void your bonus and any winnings.
