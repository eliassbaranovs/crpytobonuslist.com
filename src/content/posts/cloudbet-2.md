# CloudBet Casino Review

Its primary appeal lies in its deep game library, provably fair original titles, and a loyalty program built around continuous rakeback, though potential payment fees and mandatory KYC for withdrawals are notable drawbacks.

### Deposits and Withdrawals

CloudBet’s banking system is designed for cryptocurrency users but also accommodates traditional fiat players. The platform's flexibility in payments is one of its core strengths, supporting a wide array of digital assets across multiple blockchain networks.

The casino accepts a broad selection of over 15 cryptocurrencies. Major coins like Bitcoin (BTC) and Ethereum (ETH) are present, alongside popular stablecoins such as Tether (USDT) and USD Coin (USDC). The platform also supports fast and low-cost networks, with coins like Solana (SOL), Tron (TRX), and Cardano (ADA) available. This extensive support makes it an excellent choice for players who hold diverse crypto portfolios. For those looking for top-tier options, CloudBet ranks among the leading **[Ethereum Casinos](/ethereum-casinos/)**.

Minimum deposit amounts are generally low and accessible. For example, the minimum for Bitcoin is 0.0001 BTC, while for USDT it is 20. The deposit process is straightforward: select your currency and network, copy the provided wallet address, and send the funds. Transactions are credited automatically after the required number of network confirmations.

#### Withdrawal Speed

CloudBet processes crypto withdrawals instantly after internal approval, which can take anywhere from 0 to 2 days. For fully verified users, there is no daily withdrawal limit, a major advantage for high rollers. However, unverified accounts are capped at a relatively low limit of approximately $2,200 per day. This policy strongly encourages users to complete identity verification.

It is critical to understand that CloudBet enforces a Know Your Customer (KYC) policy. The casino can request identity verification documents, such as a passport or ID and a proof of address, before processing any withdrawal. This verification process typically adds 24-48 hours to the initial withdrawal request. Therefore, a player's first cashout could realistically take between 24 and 96 hours (1-4 days) to complete. This delay is a significant factor for players expecting immediate access to their funds. #### Fees

A notable drawback is that CloudBet reserves the right to apply handling fees of up to 5% on both deposits and withdrawals. While not always applied, the possibility of such a significant fee is a considerable negative. Most top-tier crypto casinos absorb network fees or charge a minimal, fixed fee for withdrawals. ### Bonuses and Promotions

CloudBet takes a different approach to bonuses, focusing more on long-term value through rakeback and loyalty rewards rather than a large, upfront match bonus. This model may appeal to consistent players but could be underwhelming for those seeking immediate bonus funds.

#### Welcome Bonus

The welcome package at CloudBet is structured as a 10% rakeback offer on net losses incurred during your first 30 days, capped at a total of $2,500. This is combined with daily cash drops. To activate the offer, a player only needs to make a deposit of any amount. However, to withdraw any winnings associated with the bonus rewards, a minimum lifetime deposit of at least 1 mBTC (or currency equivalent) is required, and the initial deposit must be wagered at least once.

This rakeback model means the bonus value is directly tied to a player's performance. It acts as a safety net rather than a bankroll booster. While the headline value of $2,500 is high, a player would need to sustain significant net losses to claim the full amount. For bonus wagering, slots typically contribute 100%, while live casino and table games contribute only 10%, which is a standard industry practice.

#### Ongoing Promotions

The real value at CloudBet comes from its ongoing promotions for existing players. The platform leans heavily on its loyalty program, which provides continuous rakeback on every bet placed, starting at a baseline rate and increasing with a player's tier. During special "TURBO" windows, this rakeback can reach as high as 25%.

In addition to rakeback, players benefit from daily, weekly, and monthly cash drops, which are automatically credited based on recent activity. These rewards must be claimed from a rewards calendar within a specific timeframe. The casino also frequently hosts network tournaments from providers like Pragmatic Play ("Drops & Wins"), offering massive prize pools shared across participating casinos. While CloudBet doesn't offer a steady stream of traditional reload bonuses or free spin packages, its system of consistent rakeback and frequent prize drops provides sustained value for regular bettors.
