## Licensing and Security

Rakebit is owned and operated by Innovex Tech Holdings Ltd and holds a gaming license from the Anjouan Gaming Board in the Union of Comoros. The license number is ALSI-202504026-FI1, and its status can be verified on the regulator's public register. The casino's ownership and licensing information are transparently displayed on its website.

It is crucial for players to understand the context of an Anjouan license. This jurisdiction has become a popular alternative for operators following the 2023-2024 reforms of the Curacao licensing system. However, Anjouan is considered a light-touch regulator. It provides minimal oversight and offers very limited avenues for player dispute resolution compared to top-tier authorities like the Malta Gaming Authority (MGA) or the UK Gambling Commission (UKGC). This means players have fewer protections if a conflict arises with the casino.

In terms of technical security, the platform uses standard encryption to protect user data. For game fairness, Rakebit relies on Provably Fair technology for its original games, which allows for cryptographic verification of game outcomes. Games from third-party providers are subject to their own independent RNG (Random Number Generator) testing and certification.
