The Costa Rican government does not regulate online gambling, offers no player protection, and provides no mechanism for dispute resolution. To understand why licensing matters and how it affects player safety, see our guide on **[is crypto gambling safe](/guides/is-crypto-gambling-safe)**.

It offers minimal oversight and player protection, falling far short of the standards set by top-tier regulators like the Malta Gaming Authority (MGA) or the UK Gambling Commission (UKGC). The lack of a credible license means players have little to no recourse if they encounter a dispute with the casino.
