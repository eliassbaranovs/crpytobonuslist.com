# Spree Casino Review

Spree Casino is a strong contender in the US sweepstakes market due to its massive game library and reputable licensing, but its slow redemption process holds it back from being a top-tier choice.

Launched in 2024 by Play Spree Ltd, Spree Casino operates under the sweepstakes model, making it legally accessible in 35 US states. It holds a license from the Isle of Man Gambling Supervision Commission, a respected regulatory body that provides a higher level of oversight than many competitors. The platform's main appeal is its extensive collection of over 2,000 games from leading developers like Pragmatic Play and NetEnt, combined with a generous no-deposit bonus of 25,000 Gold Coins and 2.5 free Spree Coins upon registration. While the game variety and promotional structure are excellent, players must contend with the absence of live chat support and a prize redemption timeline that can stretch to over two weeks for first-time bank transfers.

The majority of the library consists of slots. Players will find popular video slots such as *Wolf Gold*, *Sugar Rush*, and *Gates of Olympus* from Pragmatic Play, alongside classic three-reel slots like *Burning Wins: Classic 5 Lines* and *777 Fruity Classic*. ### Deposits and Withdrawals

As a sweepstakes casino, Spree Casino does not handle real-money deposits in the traditional sense. Instead, players purchase packages of Gold Coins (GC), which are virtual credits for gameplay only. These purchases often come with free promotional Spree Coins (SC) as a bonus. Spree Coins hold a redemption value of 1 SC to $1 USD and can be exchanged for cash prizes or gift cards once the playthrough requirements are met.

#### Purchase Methods

Players can purchase Gold Coin packages using a limited but standard set of payment options. The casino accepts major credit and debit cards, including Visa and Mastercard. For mobile users, Apple Pay and Google Pay are also supported, providing convenient and secure transaction methods. All transactions are processed in USD. The casino does not disclose any processing fees for these purchases. This selection is adequate for most users but lacks the diversity of payment methods found at many international online casinos.

#### Withdrawal Speed

Redeeming prizes at Spree Casino is a multi-step process with a notably long timeline. Before any redemption is possible, players must play through their Spree Coins at least once (1x playthrough). The minimum redemption amount is $75, equivalent to 75 SC. The platform offers two redemption methods: bank transfer (ACH) and gift cards. Gift card redemptions are processed within 48 hours, making them the faster option.

Bank transfers, however, are subject to a much longer waiting period. Spree Casino states a processing time of 3-10 business days for ACH transfers. This does not include the internal review period. For a player's first redemption, a mandatory Know Your Customer (KYC) verification is required, which can take up to 72 hours. The casino also has a separate pending period of up to 72 hours for reviewing the redemption request itself. When combined, a first-time bank transfer redemption could realistically take between 8 and 16 business days to complete (72 hours KYC + 72 hours pending + 10 business days transfer). Players seeking quicker access to their winnings may find this delay unacceptable. Its standards are comparable to top-tier authorities like the Malta Gaming Authority (MGA), placing Spree Casino on a higher footing of trustworthiness than platforms licensed in jurisdictions like Curacao or Anjouan.
