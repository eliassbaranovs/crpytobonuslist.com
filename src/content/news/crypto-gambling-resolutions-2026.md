## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is bankroll management in crypto gambling?

Bankroll management involves setting limits on how much you're willing to lose and when to stop playing. It helps prevent impulsive decisions and protects your funds over time.

### How does the house edge affect my gambling results?

The house edge is the casino's advantage over the player. Even a small edge can lead to significant losses over time, making it crucial to choose games with lower edges for better long-term results.

### Why is the provably fair system important?

The provably fair system allows players to verify the fairness of game outcomes. It ensures transparency and builds trust between the player and the platform, protecting your bankroll.

### How can I avoid chasing losses?

To avoid chasing losses, set strict limits and stick to them. Recognize when you're on a losing streak and take a break to reassess your strategy, rather than trying to win back losses immediately.
