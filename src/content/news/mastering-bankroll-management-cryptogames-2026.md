CryptoGames offers a thrilling BTC gaming experience, but effective bankroll management is crucial to sustain long-term play. Here's how you can manage your bankroll and enjoy the ride at CryptoGames without losing your shirt.

## What Happened

CryptoGames has integrated features to help players manage their bankrolls more effectively. The platform's built-in statistics tab allows users to track their play history, providing insights into their gaming patterns. This tool is vital for setting realistic betting limits and maintaining control over your gambling sessions.

By treating your BTC deposit as your total bankroll, not just your per-session budget, you can chop it into smaller units. This approach helps you weather the inevitable ups and downs of gambling without wiping out your funds in a single night.

## Impact and Reaction

Players at CryptoGames are encouraged to set both loss and win limits before starting a session. This strategy is crucial for maintaining discipline, especially during winning streaks. Walking away when ahead ensures you're in the game longer, preventing emotional decisions that could lead to chasing losses.

The emotional aspect of bankroll management can't be overstated. Chasing losses during a losing streak can deplete your balance quickly. By sticking to your limits, you protect your bankroll and keep gambling fun rather than stressful.

### How can I track my gambling statistics on CryptoGames?
