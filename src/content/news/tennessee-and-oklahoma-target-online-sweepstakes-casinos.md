# Tennessee and Oklahoma Target Online Sweepstakes Casinos

## The Details
Tennessee and Oklahoma are cracking down on online sweepstakes casinos. Both states have passed legislation in their senates to classify these platforms as illegal gambling. This targets dual-currency models that many online casinos use. The legislation now moves to the House for further debate. This step is part of a broader effort by multiple states to regulate unlicensed online gaming.

## Market & Player Impact
For players, this means tighter controls on sweepstakes platforms in these states. Crypto casinos using dual-currency models might face scrutiny. This could limit game access or even block services in Tennessee and Oklahoma. The new rules focus on consumer protection by tightening unregulated gambling operations.

## What Happens Next
1. Watch for the House votes in Tennessee and Oklahoma.
2. Expect increased KYC/AML checks in these states.
3. Casinos may need to adjust dual-currency offerings.
4. Players might see restricted access to certain games.
5. Monitor for similar actions in other states.
