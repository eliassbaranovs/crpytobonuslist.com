Ethereum gas fees dropped below 5 gwei this week. That is the lowest since early 2024. A standard ETH transfer now costs under $0.50. ERC-20 token transfers like USDT or USDC run about $1-2.

For crypto casino players, this changes the math on which chain to use for deposits.

## Why It Matters

When gas fees were at 30-50 gwei last year, depositing $100 in ETH cost $5-15 in fees. That is a 5-15% tax before you even play. Most players switched to TRC-20 USDT or BNB chain alternatives.

At 5 gwei, a $100 ETH deposit costs about $0.40 in gas. That puts Ethereum back in the running as a practical deposit method for regular-sized transactions.

## What Caused the Drop

Two things are driving the low fees:

1. Layer 2 adoption pulled a lot of activity off mainnet Ethereum. Arbitrum and Base handle the bulk of DeFi transactions now.
2. The Dencun upgrade from 2024 continues to reduce blob costs, and the recent Pectra upgrade further optimized execution layer efficiency.

Less competition for block space means cheaper transactions for everyone still using mainnet.
